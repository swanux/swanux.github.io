App Spotlight

Do you remember the first time you've seen a Linux desktop? Years, decades, or just some days ago? If the answer is yes, then I guess that you still remember the feeling after coming from Mac, or especially Windows: Now what? Where can I get my favourite browser? Where is the offie suite? And so on...

App Spotlight is like something that you've probably seen before in several distros: browser switcher. However, this small program can do more: Office, Editor, Communication, Games, etc.

So if you're new to Linux, or just would like to install the most common applications (which sometimes are not available from the default repos) with ease, then it's the perfect place fro you.
