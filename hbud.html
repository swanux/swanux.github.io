HBud

Simple music / video player and karaoke app written in Python and GTK - Because why not

What is the goal?

First of all, to have fun.

Also, the goal is to have a simple to use, and simple to write audio and video player on Linux. I was looking into the source code of lots of similar programs and when I saw the dozens of separate files, custom written widgets and industry standard hence less intuitive coding I thought it is too complicated for a simple program.

So if you're learning programming (Python3, GTK3+, GStreamer, multi-threading, etc) it's an easy to edit example. Feel free to play with it.
